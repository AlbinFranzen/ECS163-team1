Global Migration Flows Dashboard

This dashboard visualizes international migration patterns between countries and regions for the periods 1990-1995, 1995-2000, 2000-2005, and 2005-2010. Click on a country on the map to see its specific migration flows and trends. The line chart displays flows over time, and the chord diagram illustrates inter-regional relationships.
